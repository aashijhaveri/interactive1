Prelude

Graphic Designer

Yuna is a sophmore Graphic design transfer student from Seattle. She is originally from South Korea.

Interview

  • How is art making offline different than art making offline?

    • Art making online has more options to consider. We have several different display sizes, such as mobile phones which are mostly vertical display and laptops that are mostly horizontal display.
    Art making offline is usually done with the first format that artist create.


  • How has your view of the internet / how you experience being online changed over time?

    • I think the design of the display is becoming simple. Designers are leaving more blank spaces/ empty spaces on the page. Also, there are more icons to use space well compared to using all words. Using the empty space has become more important.


  • How does the Web inpact Graphic Design?

    • Designers have to think more about the quality of the images. Also, the choice of a typeface is important because it has to be legible, yet attractive to the eye.
